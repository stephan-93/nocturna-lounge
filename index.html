Discover your Alter Ego

Nocturna Lounge is Hawaii’s first NextGen lounge, providing a stylish and state-of-the-art karaoke experience, next generation video game consoles featuring the latest in social gaming, and a full bar with a unique selection of specialty cocktails. Conveniently located with ample parking at Waterfront Plaza (formerly Restaurant Row), we’re open 7 days a week until 2 am. Spacious suites are also available for private parties and corporate events.
